Nick’s Tournament Tool

This website uses cookies

It stores the cookie “nicktt_currenttournament” that lasts for 100 days when you click on the “New Tournament” button.

It also stores the same cookie when you click on the “Load Tournament” button.

This cookie is used to determine which tournament the website should display.

Clicking “New” will clear the current tournament and generate a new tournament-key.

Your previous tournaments can be accessed by clicking on the "Load Tournament" button and entering their tournament-key.
